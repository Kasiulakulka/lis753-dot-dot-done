Dot Dot Done

Bullet journaling is a personalized system for recording all the things that matter most to you in a single journal. My journey with bullet journals started when:

  • I was sitting at my desk at work writing down logins and passwords on sticky notes.
  • I was at home writing in my journal about my day.
  • I was jotting down my grocery list on a whiteboard on my fridge and transferring all of that onto a small list.
  • I was creating random notes about things I want to do in the future on scrap papers.
  • I was typing excel sheets to keep up bill due dates.​

I was all over the place!

I was losing those sticky notes and scrap papers. What was the password to that site I visited two years ago? When do I pay that electric bill? Ugh! How could I keep track of everything?

Simple. One journal.

A journal that is:

  1. sturdy enough to travel with me wherever I go.
  2. has thick paper to be able to use pens, highlighters, and markers without any bleeding.
  3. folds flat to be able to draw lines with a ruler for those calendars I wanted to create.
  4. has numbers on the corners to be able to keep track of where I jotted down what I'm looking for.

After finding that perfect journal, and putting in a little time to make it my own, suddenly those late fees disappeared. Last-minute gifts became a thing of the past. Remembering to stop by the store to pick up toilet paper on my way home saved some dignity! Ladies and gents, I could get used to this!

After my success, I want to share the power of a bullet journal with the world!
