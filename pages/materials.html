Materials

Making your bullet journal pretty isn't necessary, but it is fun! All you really need is a journal and a pen or pencil, but many people choose to go the extra mile and splurge on fancy items to make their journal unique.

  • A journal of your choosing. You can find all kinds of journals on Amazon in different prices ranges.
  • A pencil, mechanical or otherwise
  • A pen. Watch out for the different ballpoint sizes in millimeters as well as the type of ink in them (gel, water-based, or oil-based). I find that gel pens are my least favorite because they smear easily.
  • Highlighters
  • A ruler
  • Fun washi-tape to decorate borders or to create lists with a fun heading
  • Stickers!
  • A good eraser
  • Correction tape
  • Binder clips
  • Sticky notes
  • Pencil sharpener

Colors can make your pages pop!
